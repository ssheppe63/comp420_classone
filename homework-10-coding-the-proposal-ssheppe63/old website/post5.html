day 2 -- carrots with hookah

8-17-16 12:13 AM

My day started at 4 am! And then again at 3 pm. Whoops. Guess jet lag did happen. My sweet roommates came back for me after they ate lunch to make sure I was alive and we spent our afternoon on the island between Buda and Pest, Margaret Island. It's absolutely beautiful and had tons of weird, cool things like a mini zoo (including deers, rabbits, ducks, turtles and horses). We explored the island, walked from one bridge to the other and met up with most of the AIT kids that are here for the langauge school at the most popular ruin bar, Szimpla Kert. This included carrots and hookah, classic combination.
