weeks 1 & 2: staying out till 4 am

8-27-16 5:56 AM

I'm a bit late in writing this, 2 weeks or so but I take that as a good sign that I'm too busy to spend time on my computer. Preceding my first day of the Babilon language school I didn't sleep. My roommates and I were 40 minutes lost, but still 10 minutes early thank god and then I briefly fell asleep in class with the teacher who hated us. Brief overviews: awesome foodtruck area by Szimpla, playing frisbee for hours at Margaret, staying out till 4 am and not remembering the majority of the night besides apparently doing some weird dance, staying out till 4 am the next two nights dancing for hours, awkward racist(?) lunch, St. Stephen's day -- amazing food and fireworks, my first time to Ikea, Carolyn's weird shuffling, Hungarian folk dancing class, City Park and more frisbee, making the crazy sandwich with 3 weird types of meet and wasabi cheese, Gellert Hill walking tour, amazing chocolate raspberry icecream, skipping class and going to the Gastro Pub. Life and everything is so amazing here, I never want to leave. I do want my student pass though so I can stop riding the tram in semi fear of getting a ticket for not punching. I also wish the go to vegetarian option wasn't fried cheese, but it's still delicious. Mo and I had ridiculous "cheeseburgers" which were just fried cheese instead of meat at the food truck area in Szimpla. I ventured out and bought white sneakers (from H&M, nothing too crazy) and I think we've tried every Bubble Tea place in the city. So far all the teas are way too sweet, it's just fruit syrup and the bubbles are a hit or miss. We're hopeful though... I'm into red bean now.
